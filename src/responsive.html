Responsive layout

Screen detection:
Current screen of size indicates: XL (extra large) LG (large) MD (medium) SM (small) XS (extra small)

Following media break points are supported:

  • xl for extra large displays/devices (such as widescreen desktops)
  • lg for large displays/devices (desktops/tablets)
  • md for medium displays/devices (tablets/smartphones)
  • sm for large displays/devices (smartphones)
  • xs for extra small displays/devices (old smartphones)
The breakpoint sizes are defined in theme-constants.scss
